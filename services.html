Product engineering centers around innovation. It is important for organizations to utilize product engineering solutions for the deployment of their software products. Accelerate and improve innovation, market delivery, and provide a top-notch customer experience.

Product Engineering can be defined as the development and design of a product. Typically it is the entire development, design, deployment, modernization, and innovation involved in a product. In earlier times, product engineering was considered as the development of a realistic physical product, however, today, it is considered as custom software development.Product engineering services utilize software, hardware, and IT services solutions for the development and design of a product.

Business process reengineering is a systemic approach to improving the efficiency of an organization by improvements in (appropriate) processes in such areas as cost, operation, speed, service and quality. This drastic change process begins at the highest level of a business and goes down to the littlest detail to redesign and overhaul the system in no time.

As a total redesign, PR varies from other processes where gradual improvement comes from frequent process upgrades. Organizations going through PR must re-examine their fundamentals and change their methodology with the aim of standardization and simplification.Simplify3X reduces the organizational layers and removes activities that are unproductive in two major areas.
