Q: What is SimplifyQA?

SimplifyQA provides an interoperable definition of cloud services and its applications, including the components, relationships, requirements, dependencies, and capabilities, enabling the automated management and portability of cloud providers, regardless of their platform or infrastructure, consequently it enhances consumer preference, better reliability, and reduces expenses and time. These characteristic features also facilitate portable and continuous delivery of applications (DevOps) throughout the entire life-cycle. In short, they give the cloud business a greater level of accuracy and agility.

SimplifyQA equips businesses in capturing, automating and optimizing the use of expert information in order to automatically match service and application requirements to the correct cloud service provider capability, thereby allowing a truly competitive environment in which cloud platforms and service providers can conquer commercialization to compete, develop, and better meet the demands of cloud-based companies.
